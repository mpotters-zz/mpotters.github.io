ADVERTISING PRODUCTION

Integrated Producer // Technologist

  • Role:
    Digital Producer
  • Date:
    Summer 2015
  • Client:
    MasterCard

In partnership with SU2C, MasterCard launched a Priceless Surprises campaign that encouraged cardholders to donate to cancer research through pop-up dinners by celebrity chefs in alternative venues. The digital side of the campaign handled elements like event details, dynamic rendering of reservation requests, and other campaign information. Constructed with a global development team and on-site production, over one summer the project raised several million dollars.

WRITING + THINKING

Media Theory // Technological Critique

  • Title:
    Information Value & Digital Creative Industry
  • Publication:
    NYU

This thesis explores an important history: the legal lineage of copyright and intellectual property in the modern form of SOPA and PIPA; it examines their impacts on the creative industries that have emerged on the Internet. Through this examination emerges a novel understanding of how these industries function in modern digital economies. Only through studying this tension - or this failed history - can scholars and policy makers construct practical legal parameters for the modern Internet.

  • Title:
    Digital Listening: Reproduced Musical Work
  • Publication:
    Wesleyan University

This thesis situates the musical work of art within a new technological age, through an analysis of the musical work of art, of listening types and their relation to socio-economic structures, of media study, and of musicological ramifications. It paves the way to an understanding of the increasingly-difficult-to-find authentic listening experience. It studies and validates the contemporary musical work in its many technological manifestations.

MEDIA ART

Front End Dev // Sound Experiments

  • Title:
    cl!ck
  • Role:
    Composer // Developer
  • Tools:
    Ableton // Max // Web MIDI

Currently in development, cl!ck is a multimedia installation that comments on digital life in the 21st Century; specifically, the piece explores our choices on social media as an indication of personality, cultural tendencies, and subconscious decision making. The piece is a mirror of Facebook's (and other social networks') Newsfeed algorithm, wherein user selections influence the content displayed. Including the development of a web app, this piece features original choreography, interactive visual elements, and a modular score.

In partnership with Kate Ladenheim & Pierre Depaz. Debuting Fall 2016.

  • Title:
    CitiBach
  • Role:
    Composer // Developer
  • Tools:
    Max // tonejs

CitiBach was created, composed, and developed in an eight hour period at a Spotify Music Hackathon. Utilizing the sampling functions in the Tonejs library, our team took CitiBike location and usage data, and mapped a generative piece that reveals itself as CitiBike usage increases through the morning rush. In so doing we beta tested a new js library, and painted a unique picture of ridership around New York.
